# Terms And Conditions 

## Overview

The Terms And Conditions component displays comprehensive legal information about the company's terms of service, product usage policies, and customer agreements. The component is structured with clear sections covering various legal aspects of using the website and purchasing products, ensuring customers understand their rights and obligations.
